Who am I?

Hi, I'm an Msc student at Umeå University studying Interaction Technology and Design. I was born in Uppsala, Sweden in 1995. I grew up in a family of five, we had a lot of music around and singing is still a big part of my day to day life.
Before heading off to university, I spent a year selling laser systems at a company called Gammadata Instrument. During my time with them I got to travel round Europe meeting customers and attending trade fairs. I've just finished my third year at university.
This past year has been a lot of fun. I've studied web design and database design, both SQL and NoSQL.
I've also been the project leader of the DataTjej Conference, a project where we focus on working towards equality within the IT-buisness. Letting women and non-binary IT-students network among each other and with companies within the industry. Outside school I enjoy training, hanging out with friends and singing in a choir.

Project manager for the DataTjej Conference 2019

DataTjej is a non-profit organization working to promote women and non-binary people of all ages who are interested in, studying or working with IT. DataTjej is probably best known for the annual DataTjej Conference, where companies and students get the opportunity to network. In 2019 the conferance was held in Umeå and I was the project manager and together with a team om 12 women we planned and delivered a three day long conference.
For more information on what DataTjej do, visit their website DataTjej.se
